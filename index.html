Résumé

I am a Research Engineer at Google Research, in Mountain View, CA. I am currently working on Camera Autofocus for Pixel Phones and Neural Structured Learning.

Previously, I was a Master's candidate in Computer Science at Stanford University and an Undergraduate Student in Computer Science at IIT Kharagpur, India.

My research interests lie within machine learning for computer vision. I have previously worked on data augmentation, representation learning (self-supervised | unsupervised | supervised), generative modeling and applications of machine learning to electic areas like augmented reality, fashion commerce, medical imaging, computational sustainability, and computational biology. In a past life, I also did some work on natural language and computer networks. Check out my research page for more.
